About Me !

Dedicated artist and advocate for sustainable development, committed to creating meaningful change through creative expression and inclusive education. As a Project Coordinator and Workshop Facilitator, she specializes in empowering individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities through innovative skill-building programs. Her work spans art therapy using eco-friendly materials, sexual awareness education for all age groups, and sustainable product design. With a deep belief in community impact and ethical practice, Soumi bridges art, education, and sustainability to foster environments where every individual can thrive and contribute to a better world.
