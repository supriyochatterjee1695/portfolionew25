Who we are

Sankalp Global is the social impact initiative of Sankalp, dedicated to education-focused sustainable development and community capacity building.

Foundational learning

Strengthening literacy, numeracy, and life skills through contextual content.

SDG-linked modules

Embedding climate, gender, and sustainability themes in the curriculum.

Community partnerships

Working with parents, educators, and local bodies to keep learning continuous.

Education programs

Programmes are designed to be modular, measurable, and adaptable to different learning environments.

School-based SDG labs

Experiential learning modules delivered in schools to help learners link classroom concepts with real-world sustainability challenges.

  • Activity-based learning kits
  • Teacher facilitation guides
  • Simple assessment tools

Community learning circles

Curated sessions in communities that blend storytelling, local knowledge, and digital content to keep learners engaged.

  • Weekend and after-school formats
  • Parent and youth volunteers
  • Offline-friendly content

Educator capacity building

Workshops and continuous support for teachers on facilitation, digital tools, and SDG-linked pedagogy.

Impact measurement & research

Simple frameworks and tools to capture learning outcomes, engagement, and community feedback.

Digital learning ecosystem

A lightweight LMS and Android app extend learning beyond the classroom and keep learners connected.

  • LMS with structured courses, assessments, and progress tracking.
  • Android app for low-bandwidth content, quizzes, and notifications.
  • Spaces to integrate partner content, videos, and local language resources.
